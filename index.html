About

I'm a computer scientist and researcher based in Dubai, UAE — with deep expertise in AI, large language models, aviation analytics, and enterprise AI integration.

My work bridges rigorous academic research and real-world engineering. I publish in Frontiers, IEEE, and Springer while also building AI tools for aviation safety teams and automating complex workflows through RPA systems.

Currently a Research Assistant at the Aviation Center of Excellence, University of Sharjah, collaborating with GCAA and airline partners on intelligent flight data systems.

Beyond research, I'm a graphic designer and visual communicator who has created campaigns for Dubai Police, aviation research brochures, event materials, and animated awareness videos across multiple organizations. I also actively mentor research assistants and interns, having guided 53+ individuals through research projects.

Projects

✈ Aviation AI
FDR Anomaly Detection System

AI system for detecting anomalies in Flight Data Recorder multivariate time-series. Built a reproducible pipeline with synthetic anomaly injection evaluating multiple unsupervised models on ~56,000 rows of ~100 flight parameters in collaboration with GCAA.

Models: Isolation Forest, One-Class SVM, LOF, DBSCAN, GMM, Autoencoders. Custom evaluation metrics: Detection@k and FAR@k.

PythonScikit-learnTime-SeriesAnomaly DetectionGCAA Collaboration
⚙ RPA & Automation
CORISA Fuel Optimization Web App

Built a web application to automate fuel-related workflows for an aviation project in collaboration with Etihad Airways and GCAA. The system streamlines CORISA fuel data processing, reducing manual effort through automated pipelines and a purpose-built front-end interface.
