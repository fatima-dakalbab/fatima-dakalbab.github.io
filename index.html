About

I'm a computer scientist and researcher based in Sharjah, UAE — with deep expertise in AI, large language models, aviation analytics, and enterprise AI integration.

My work bridges rigorous academic research and real-world engineering — publishing in Frontiers, IEEE, and Springer while also deploying on-premise LLMs into ERP systems and building AI tools for aviation safety.

Currently at the Aviation Center of Excellence, University of Sharjah — collaborating with GCAA and airline partners on intelligent flight data systems and aviation AI tools.

Beyond research, I'm a graphic designer and visual communicator — I've created branding campaigns for Dubai Police, aviation brochures, roll-up banners, event posters, animated awareness videos, and dozens of academic research visuals across multiple organizations.

Projects

✈ Flagship · Aviation AI
FDR Anomaly Detection System

AI system for detecting anomalies in Flight Data Recorder multivariate time-series. Reproducible pipeline with synthetic anomaly injection evaluating multiple unsupervised models on ~56,000 rows / ~100 flight parameters.

Models: Isolation Forest, One-Class SVM, LOF, DBSCAN, GMM, Autoencoders. Custom metrics: Detection@k and FAR@k.

LLM Integration into ERPNext

Deployed GPT-Neo 125M (8-bit quantized) on-premise into ERPNext via custom Python backend with Frappe framework. Full chat interface, backend pipelines, on-premise deployment — enterprise AI without cloud dependency.
